BUSINESS OUTLINE

ANYTHINGHOME PTY LTD takes pride in having young South African male as its Managing Director and is hundred percent black owned. One of our focuses as a company is to empower the previously disadvantaged individuals by giving them an opportunity to acquire experience and develop their skills through our business so they can ultimately reach their fullest potential. We do believe in equal opportunities, hence, we give opportunities to both male and female so as to not only build them, but share diferent skills and expertise to further grow the business. Very soon we will offer newly graduate students an opportunity to do their practical training in our business where we nurture them and employ the raw post matriculates that are stil looking for their passion or doing a year of their lives.
